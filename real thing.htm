About us

Lives Changing Centre is a community-focused church serving Mathare and neighbouring communities. Our mission is to lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus, care for the vulnerable, and serve our neighbourhood practically.

Leaders

  • Bishop Joseph Ndungu — Chief pastor and overseer.
  • Pastor Monicah — Pastor and community programs coordinator.

Ministries

Worship

Sunday worship, choir and band ministry.

Youth

Youth meetings, mentorship and leadership training.

Children

Engaging Sunday school for kids (0–12).

Outreach

Community feeding, practical support and programmes for vulnerable families.

Prayer

Midweek prayer meetings and personal prayer teams.

Counselling

Pastoral counselling by request; confidential and compassionate.
